Our Story

Founded in 2010, Coffee Haven began as a small neighborhood cafe with a simple mission: to serve exceptional coffee in a welcoming environment. Over the years, we've grown into a community hub where people connect, create, and savor the moment.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms around the world, roast them in-house to bring out their unique flavors, and train our baristas to craft each drink with precision and care.

  • Direct trade, ethically sourced coffee beans
  • In-house roasting for optimal freshness
  • Skilled baristas with continuous training
  • Cozy atmosphere with free Wi-Fi
  • Locally sourced pastries and snacks
