About Our Park Cat

Meet Whiskers, the park's most beloved resident. This curious feline spends her days exploring every corner of the park, from the flower beds to the old oak trees.

Whiskers arrived at the park three years ago and quickly became a local favorite. She enjoys sunbathing on the benches, chasing butterflies, and greeting park visitors with gentle purrs.

Local residents often bring treats and toys for Whiskers, who has become something of a park mascot. She's particularly fond of the afternoon sun patches near the fountain.
