Brewing Excellence Since 2010

Founded by two coffee enthusiasts, Brew & Bean started as a small neighborhood cafe with a simple mission: to serve exceptional coffee in a welcoming environment.

Over the years, we've maintained our commitment to quality while expanding our offerings to include a variety of specialty drinks, teas, and homemade pastries.

We work directly with sustainable coffee farms to ensure ethical sourcing and the finest quality beans. Each batch is carefully roasted to bring out unique flavor profiles.
